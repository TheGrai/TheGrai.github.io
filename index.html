Tsunami

Tsunami, was written for the module 12 assignment. The purpose was to create a “nature beast”. The challenge was to create conflict between nature and a character. However, the nature was to have some sort of human attribute attached to it. I think I accomplished this in the small amount of words I was allotted. I really enjoyed it and think it turned out quite good. I struggled with coming up with the narrative and setting that I wanted to portray. However, once I figured it out it was pretty easy to find the right flow. There was a balance between describing the Tsunami and with pushing the story forward. The story has a nice “rollercoaster” that starts happy, goes dark, and then ends with a hopeful scene.
